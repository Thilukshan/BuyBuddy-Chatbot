Policies (data/policies/)

return_policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery.
Products must be unused, undamaged, and in the original packaging.
To start a return, contact customer support with your order ID and reason for return.

refund_policy

Once the returned item is received and inspected, refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.
The refund will be sent to the original payment method.
You will receive a confirmation message once the refund is completed.

exchange_policy

Exchanges are allowed within 14 days of delivery.
Items must be unused and in original condition.
If the requested item is out of stock, a refund will be issued instead.

shipping_policy

Standard delivery time is 3–7 business days.
Delivery time may vary based on location and availability.
Shipping delays may occur due to weather, holidays, or courier issues.

international_shipping_policy

Currently, we do not support international shipping.
We only deliver within Sri Lanka.

payment_policy

We accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile wallets, and online banking.
All payments are processed securely.
Cash on Delivery may be available for selected locations.

cancellation_policy

Orders can be canceled within 2 hours of placement.
After dispatch, cancellation is not allowed.
To cancel an order, contact customer support immediately.

Navigation / How-To (data/navigation/)

track_order

To track your order:
1.	Log in to your account
2.	Go to My Orders
3.	Select the order
4.	View shipment status and estimated delivery date

estimated_delivery

Estimated delivery time is shown in My Orders under each order.
You will also receive delivery updates via SMS or email.

change_delivery_address

You can change the delivery address before the order is shipped.
Go to My Orders → Edit Address or contact support for help.

return_product

To return a product:
1.	Log in to your account
2.	Go to My Orders
3.	Select the product
4.	Click Request Return
5.	Follow the instructions provided

reset_password

To reset your password:
1.	Go to the login page
2.	Click Forgot Password
3.	Enter your registered email
4.	Follow the reset link sent to your email

update_payment_method

To update your payment method:
1.	Log in to your account
2.	Go to Account Settings
3.	Select Payment Methods
4.	Add or update your payment details

add_shipping_address

To add a new shipping address:
1.	Log in to your account
2.	Go to Profile → Addresses
3.	Click Add New Address
4.	Save changes

apply_discount_code

To apply a discount code:
1.	Add items to your cart
2.	Go to checkout
3.	Enter the discount code in the promo box
4.	Click Apply

subscribe_newsletter

To subscribe to our newsletter:
1.	Enter your email at the bottom of our website
2.	Click Subscribe
You will receive offers, updates, and new product alerts.
